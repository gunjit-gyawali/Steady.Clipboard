## Steady.Clipboard

***Never lose your copied text again!***  
This little Chrome extension keeps your clipboard safe until *you* decide to delete it. No more accidental overwrites or losing something important when you close your browser.  

***

## How it works
1. Click the extension icon.  
2. Paste or type the text you want to save.  
3. Your text stays there no expiration, no disappearing tricks.  
4. When you’re done with it, just hit delete.  

***

## Why you’ll love it
- **Super simple** and easy to use.  
- **Text stays saved** even after closing Chrome.  
- **100% local** nothing leaves your browser.  

***

Finally, a clipboard that actually behaves the way you want.
